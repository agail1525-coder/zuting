# Pilgrimage Preparation Checklists / 朝圣准备清单

> Global Ancestral Temple Travel Platform — 全球祖庭旅行平台
> Version 1.0 | 2026-03-25

Practical checklists for visiting sacred sites of each faith tradition. These should be surfaced to users in the trip preparation flow.

各信仰传统圣地参访实用清单。应在行程准备流程中展示给用户。

## 1. Buddhism / 佛教

### What to Bring / 携带物品
- [ ] Modest long clothing (cover shoulders, knees) / 端庄长款衣物（遮盖肩膀、膝盖）
- [ ] Slip-on shoes (easy to remove frequently) / 一脚蹬鞋（方便频繁脱穿）
- [ ] Small bag for shoes / 装鞋的小袋
- [ ] Offering items: incense, flowers, candles (or purchase on-site) / 供品：香、花、烛（或现场购买）
- [ ] Meditation cushion or small mat (optional) / 禅修垫或小垫（可选）
- [ ] Water bottle (many temples are in warm climates) / 水瓶（许多寺院在温暖气候区）
- [ ] Sunscreen and hat for outdoor complexes / 户外建筑群用防晒霜和帽子
- [ ] Pocket notebook for reflections / 随身笔记本记录感悟
- [ ] Small flashlight for cave temples / 洞窟寺院用小手电

### What to Wear / 着装要求
- [ ] Long pants or skirt below the knee / 长裤或过膝裙
- [ ] Shirt covering shoulders / 遮盖肩膀的上衣
- [ ] White clothing for Theravada ceremonies (optional) / 上座部仪式穿白衣（可选）
- [ ] No transparent or tight-fitting clothing / 不穿透明或紧身衣物
- [ ] Socks (some temple floors are cold) / 袜子（部分寺院地板很冷）

### What NOT to Do / 禁止事项
- [ ] Do NOT point feet at Buddha images / 不可用脚指向佛像
- [ ] Do NOT touch monks if you are female (Theravada) / 女性不可触碰僧侣（上座部）
- [ ] Do NOT take selfies with sacred statues / 不可与神圣佛像自拍
- [ ] Do NOT step on the threshold of temple doors / 不可踩踏寺院门槛
- [ ] Do NOT bring meat or alcohol onto temple grounds / 不可携带荤腥或酒精进入寺院
- [ ] Do NOT sit higher than monks or Buddha images / 不可坐在比僧侣或佛像更高的位置
- [ ] Do NOT use flash photography in shrine rooms / 殿堂内不可使用闪光灯
- [ ] Do NOT smoke anywhere near temple grounds / 寺院范围内绝不吸烟

### Key Phrases / 常用语句
| Language / 语言 | Phrase / 语句 | Meaning / 含义 |
|---|---|---|
| Pali | Namo Buddhaya | Homage to the Buddha / 礼敬佛陀 |
| Thai | Sawasdee krap/ka | Hello / 你好 |
| Thai | Kob khun krap/ka | Thank you / 谢谢 |
| Burmese | Mingalaba | Hello / 你好 |
| Japanese | Onegaishimasu | Please (when requesting) / 拜托了 |
| Chinese | A mi tuo fo (阿弥陀佛) | Namo Amitabha / 南无阿弥陀佛 |
| Sinhala | Budu saranai | May the blessings of the Buddha be with you / 佛佑您 |

### Emergency & Health / 应急与健康
- **Thailand:** Tourist Police: 1155; Ambulance: 1669
- **Myanmar:** Emergency: 199; Tourist hotline: +95-1-378-901
- **Japan:** Emergency: 110 (Police), 119 (Ambulance/Fire)
- **China:** Emergency: 110 (Police), 120 (Ambulance)
- **India:** Emergency: 112; Tourist helpline: 1363
- **Sri Lanka:** Emergency: 119; Tourist Police: 011-242-1052
- **Nepal:** Tourist Police: 1144
- **Cambodia:** Tourist Police: 012-942-484

### Health/Safety Notes / 健康安全注意
- Tropical temples: mosquito repellent essential; risk of dengue/malaria in some areas. / 热带寺院：驱蚊液必备；某些地区有登革热/疟疾风险。
- Mountain temples (Wutai, Emei): altitude awareness; warm layers even in summer. / 山地寺院（五台山、峨眉山）：注意海拔；夏季也需保暖层。
- Hydration critical in Southeast Asian heat. / 东南亚高温须注意补水。
- Temple food may not suit all dietary needs; carry snacks if needed. / 寺院食物可能不适合所有饮食需求；必要时携带零食。

### Visa/Entry Notes / 签证入境注意
- **Thailand:** Most nationalities 30-60 day visa-free. / 大多数国籍可免签30-60天。
- **Myanmar:** E-visa required for most. / 大多数需电子签证。
- **Japan:** Visa-free for many nationalities up to 90 days. / 许多国籍可免签最长90天。
- **China:** Visa required; apply in advance. Transit visa exemptions available. / 需要签证；提前申请。有过境免签政策。
- **India:** E-visa available. / 可申请电子签证。
- **Sri Lanka:** ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization) required. / 需要电子旅行授权。
- **Nepal:** Visa on arrival available. / 可落地签。
- **Cambodia:** Visa on arrival or e-visa. / 可落地签或电子签证。

## 2. Taoism / 道教

### What to Bring / 携带物品
- [ ] Comfortable hiking shoes (many temples are on mountains) / 舒适登山鞋（许多道观在山上）
- [ ] Incense (or purchase on-site; usually three sticks) / 香（或现场购买；通常三炷）
- [ ] Rain gear (mountain weather is unpredictable) / 雨具（山区天气多变）
- [ ] Warm layers (mountain temperatures drop significantly) / 保暖衣物（山区温差大）
- [ ] Walking stick (available at many mountain gates) / 登山杖（许多山门处有售）
- [ ] Cash in small denominations for offerings / 小额现金用于供养
- [ ] Water and light snacks for mountain trails / 登山道用水和轻食

### What to Wear / 着装要求
- [ ] Comfortable layers for variable mountain weather / 适应多变山区天气的分层衣物
- [ ] Sturdy footwear with good grip / 抓地力好的结实鞋子
- [ ] Modest clothing for temple halls / 进入殿堂时的端庄衣物

### What NOT to Do / 禁止事项
- [ ] Do NOT step on the threshold of temple doors (跨过门槛, 不可踩) / 不可踩门槛
- [ ] Do NOT blow out incense — wave to extinguish / 不可吹灭香——挥动熄灭
- [ ] Do NOT touch Taoist religious statues / 不可触摸道教神像
- [ ] Do NOT photograph rituals without permission / 未经许可不可拍摄仪式
- [ ] Do NOT litter on sacred mountains / 不可在圣山上丢弃垃圾
- [ ] Do NOT disrespect Taoist priests or interrupt practices / 不可对道士不敬或打断修行

### Key Phrases / 常用语句
| Language / 语言 | Phrase / 语句 | Meaning / 含义 |
|---|---|---|
| Chinese | Wu liang tian zun (无量天尊) | Boundless Heavenly Lord (Taoist greeting) / 无量天尊（道教问候） |
| Chinese | Dao zhang hao (道长好) | Greetings, Taoist Master / 道长好 |
| Chinese | Qing wen (请问) | May I ask / 请问 |
| Chinese | Xie xie (谢谢) | Thank you / 谢谢 |

### Emergency & Health / 应急与健康
- **China:** Police: 110; Ambulance: 120; Fire: 119; Tourist complaint: 12301
- Mountain sickness is possible at high-altitude Taoist mountains (Wudang: ~1612m, Hua Shan: ~2155m). / 高海拔道教名山可能出现高山反应。
- Many mountain paths are steep with narrow steps — exercise caution, especially in rain. / 许多山路陡峭台阶窄——注意安全，尤其是雨天。
- Cable cars available at most major mountains for those with mobility issues. / 大多数名山设有缆车，方便行动不便者。

### Visa/Entry Notes / 签证入境注意
- Almost all major Taoist sites are in China. / 几乎所有主要道教场所都在中国。
- China visa required for most nationalities; 144-hour transit visa-free for some entry points. / 大多数国籍需中国签证；部分入境口岸可144小时过境免签。

## 3. Christianity / 基督教

### What to Bring / 携带物品
- [ ] Modest clothing set (no bare shoulders/knees for Catholic/Orthodox sites) / 端庄衣物套装
- [ ] Scarf/head covering for women (Orthodox churches) / 女性用围巾/头巾（东正教堂）
- [ ] Bible or prayer book (optional) / 圣经或祈祷书（可选）
- [ ] Rosary/prayer beads (Catholic) / 玫瑰经念珠（天主教，可选）
- [ ] Camera (but check flash restrictions) / 相机（但注意闪光灯限制）
- [ ] Small coins for candle lighting / 小额硬币用于点蜡烛
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes (many pilgrimage routes involve walking) / 舒适步行鞋（许多朝圣路线需步行）
- [ ] Pilgrim credential/passport for Camino de Santiago / 圣雅各之路朝圣者证书（如走此路线）

### What to Wear / 着装要求
- [ ] Cover shoulders and knees (Vatican, all Catholic and Orthodox churches) / 遮盖肩膀和膝盖
- [ ] Women: headscarf in Orthodox churches and Russian churches / 女性：在东正教和俄罗斯教堂戴头巾
- [ ] Men: remove hats inside all churches / 男性：在所有教堂内脱帽
- [ ] No shorts or miniskirts in Catholic churches / 天主教堂内不穿短裤或迷你裙
- [ ] For Ethiopian Orthodox: remove shoes / 埃塞俄比亚东正教：脱鞋

### What NOT to Do / 禁止事项
- [ ] Do NOT photograph during Mass or services / 弥撒或礼拜期间不可拍照
- [ ] Do NOT take Communion unless baptized in that tradition / 除非在该传统中受洗，不可领圣餐
- [ ] Do NOT enter restricted monastic areas / 不可进入限制区域的修道院区域
- [ ] Do NOT touch relics without permission / 未经许可不可触碰圣物
- [ ] Sistine Chapel: NO photography at all / 西斯廷教堂：完全禁止拍照
- [ ] Mount Athos: Women are NOT allowed / 阿索斯山：女性不得进入
- [ ] Do NOT eat or drink inside churches / 不可在教堂内吃喝

### Key Phrases / 常用语句
| Language / 语言 | Phrase / 语句 | Meaning / 含义 |
|---|---|---|
| Latin | Pax vobiscum | Peace be with you / 愿平安与你同在 |
| Italian | Buongiorno | Good morning / 早上好 |

| Russian | Spasibo | Thank you / 谢谢 |
| Amharic | Selam | Peace/Hello / 平安/你好 |
| Portuguese | Obrigado/Obrigada | Thank you / 谢谢 |

### Emergency & Health / 应急与健康
- **Vatican/Italy:** Emergency: 112; Ambulance: 118
- **Israel:** Police: 100; Ambulance: 101
- **Spain (Camino):** Emergency: 112; keep Camino credential updated
- **Greece:** Emergency: 112
- **UK:** Emergency: 999 or 112
- **Russia:** Emergency: 112
- **Ethiopia:** Emergency: 911
- Camino de Santiago: carry blister care kit; break in shoes before departure. / 圣雅各之路：携带水泡护理包；出发前穿合脚的鞋。
- Holy Land sites: extreme heat in summer (40°C+); carry water always. / 圣地场所：夏季极端高温（40°C+）；随时携带水。

### Visa/Entry Notes / 签证入境注意
- **Vatican:** No separate visa; within Rome, Italy. / 无需单独签证；位于罗马境内。
- **Israel:** Many nationalities visa-free for 90 days. / 许多国籍可免签90天。
- **Spain/Europe (Schengen):** 90-day visa-free for many; Schengen visa otherwise. / 许多国籍可免签90天；否则需申根签证。
- **Ethiopia:** E-visa available. / 可申请电子签证。
- **Russia:** Visa required; apply in advance. / 需要签证；提前申请。

## 4. Islam / 伊斯兰教

### What to Bring / 携带物品
- [ ] Prayer mat (or use ones provided at mosques) / 礼拜垫（或使用清真寺提供的）
- [ ] Headscarf for women (REQUIRED) / 女性头巾（必须）
- [ ] Ihram garments if performing Hajj/Umrah (2 white unstitched cloths for men) / 朝觐/副朝时的戒衣（男性用两块白色未缝制布）
- [ ] Compass or Qibla app (to find prayer direction) / 指南针或朝向应用（确定礼拜方向）
- [ ] Quran or prayer book / 古兰经或祈祷书
- [ ] Slip-on shoes / 一脚蹬鞋
- [ ] Sunscreen and sunglasses (Middle Eastern sun is intense) / 防晒霜和太阳镜（中东阳光强烈）
- [ ] Electrolyte supplements (heat and fasting) / 电解质补充（高温和斋戒）
- [ ] Copy of vaccination records (Hajj requires meningitis vaccine) / 疫苗接种记录副本（朝觐要求脑膜炎疫苗）

### What to Wear / 着装要求
- [ ] Men: long pants, shirt covering shoulders / 男性：长裤，遮盖肩膀的衬衫
- [ ] Women: loose clothing covering arms and legs; headscarf / 女性：遮盖手臂和腿部的宽松衣物；头巾
- [ ] Subdued colors preferred in mosques / 清真寺内首选素色
- [ ] No transparent or figure-revealing clothing / 不穿透明或显身形的衣物
- [ ] Clean socks (you will remove shoes) / 干净袜子（需要脱鞋）

### What NOT to Do / 禁止事项
- [ ] Do NOT enter Mecca or Medina if non-Muslim (strictly enforced) / 非穆斯林不可进入麦加或麦地那（严格执行）
- [ ] Do NOT eat, drink, or smoke in public during Ramadan daylight hours (in Muslim countries) / 斋月白天不可在公共场所进食、饮水或吸烟（在穆斯林国家）
- [ ] Do NOT bring pork products or alcohol to Islamic sites / 不可携带猪肉制品或酒精至伊斯兰场所
- [ ] Do NOT walk in front of someone praying / 不可从正在祈祷的人前面走过
- [ ] Do NOT photograph women without permission / 未经许可不可拍摄女性
- [ ] Do NOT use the left hand for greetings or eating / 不可用左手问候或进食
- [ ] Do NOT display PDA (public displays of affection) / 不可公开表示亲密
- [ ] Do NOT point the soles of your feet at people or the Quran / 不可将脚底朝向他人或古兰经

### Emergency & Health / 应急与健康
- **Saudi Arabia:** Emergency: 911; Ambulance: 997
- **Turkey:** Emergency: 112
- **UAE:** Emergency: 999; Ambulance: 998
- **Morocco:** Emergency: 19 (Police), 15 (Ambulance)
- **Iran:** Emergency: 110 (Police), 115 (Ambulance)
- **Egypt:** Emergency: 122 (Police), 123 (Ambulance)
- Hajj health: extreme heat (50°C+), massive crowds, stampede risk — follow official guidance strictly. / 朝觐健康：极端高温（50°C+），大规模人群，踩踏风险——严格遵守官方指导。
- Meningitis vaccination required for Hajj visa. / 朝觐签证要求脑膜炎疫苗接种。
- Dehydration is the #1 health risk at Middle Eastern sites. / 脱水是中东场所的首要健康风险。

- **Turkey:** E-visa or visa-free for many nationalities. / 许多国籍可电子签证或免签。
- **UAE:** Visa-free or visa on arrival for many. / 许多国籍可免签或落地签。
- **Iran:** Visa required; political sensitivities — check current advisories. / 需要签证；有政治敏感性——查看当前旅行建议。
- **Morocco:** Visa-free for many nationalities up to 90 days. / 许多国籍可免签最长90天。

## 5. Hinduism / 印度教

### What to Bring / 携带物品
- [ ] Modest clothing (preferably Indian-style: salwar kameez or sari for women) / 端庄衣物（女性首选印度风格：旁遮普套装或纱丽）
- [ ] Offerings: flowers (marigolds), coconut, incense, sweets / 供品：花（万寿菊）、椰子、香、甜食
- [ ] Small bag for shoes and leather items / 鞋子和皮革物品的小袋
- [ ] Change of clothes (some rituals involve water) / 替换衣物（某些仪式涉及水）
- [ ] Waterproof bag (for Ganges/river rituals) / 防水袋（用于恒河/河流仪式）
- [ ] Mosquito repellent / 驱蚊液
- [ ] Hand sanitizer and wet wipes / 洗手液和湿巾
- [ ] Small bills/coins for temple donations and prasad / 小额钞票/硬币用于寺庙捐赠和圣食

### What to Wear / 着装要求
- [ ] Conservative clothing covering shoulders and knees / 遮盖肩膀和膝盖的保守衣物
- [ ] Remove ALL leather items before entering temples (some temples) / 进入某些寺庙前移除所有皮革物品
- [ ] South Indian temples: men may need to be bare-chested with dhoti / 南印度寺庙：男性可能需裸上身着多提
- [ ] Avoid black clothing at auspicious occasions / 吉祥场合避免黑色衣物

### What NOT to Do / 禁止事项
- [ ] Do NOT bring leather items into temples (belts, bags, shoes) / 不可携带皮革物品进入寺庙
- [ ] Do NOT discuss or serve beef EVER at Hindu sites / 绝不可在印度教场所讨论或供应牛肉
- [ ] Do NOT touch sacred items with left hand / 不可用左手触碰神圣物品
- [ ] Do NOT point feet at deities or people / 不可将脚指向神像或他人
- [ ] Do NOT enter inner sanctums unless invited / 除非受邀不可进入内殿
- [ ] Do NOT photograph inner sanctum / 不可拍摄内殿
- [ ] Non-Hindus may not be allowed in some temples (e.g., Jagannath Puri) / 非印度教徒可能不被允许进入某些寺庙
- [ ] Do NOT use the swastika out of Hindu context / 不可在印度教语境外使用卍字符

### Key Phrases / 常用语句
| Language / 语言 | Phrase / 语句 | Meaning / 含义 |
|---|---|---|
| Hindi | Namaste | I bow to the divine in you / 我向你内在的神性致敬 |
| Hindi | Jai Shri Ram / Jai Shri Krishna | Glory to Lord Ram/Krishna / 颂赞罗摩/克里希那 |
| Hindi | Dhanyavad | Thank you / 谢谢 |
| Hindi | Kripaya | Please / 请 |
| Sanskrit | Om Namah Shivaya | Salutations to Lord Shiva / 礼敬湿婆 |
| Tamil | Vanakkam | Greetings / 你好 |
| Nepali | Namaskar | Respectful greeting / 恭敬的问候 |

### Emergency & Health / 应急与健康
- **India:** Emergency: 112; Tourist helpline: 1363
- **Nepal:** Tourist Police: 1144; Ambulance: 102
- **Indonesia (Bali):** Emergency: 112
- Varanasi and river sites: waterborne illness risk — do not swallow river water. / 瓦拉纳西和河流场所：水传疾病风险——不可吞咽河水。
- Kedarnath and high-altitude temples: altitude sickness risk. / 凯达纳特和高海拔寺庙：高山反应风险。
- India heat: May-June temperatures can exceed 45°C; carry oral rehydration salts. / 印度高温：五六月温度可超45°C；携带口服补液盐。
- Food safety: drink only bottled water; eat freshly cooked food. / 食品安全：只喝瓶装水；吃新鲜烹调的食物。

## 6. Judaism / 犹太教

### What to Bring / 携带物品
- [ ] Kippah/yarmulke for men (disposable ones available at Western Wall) / 男性用小圆帽（西墙有一次性的提供）
- [ ] Modest clothing / 端庄衣物
- [ ] Written prayers/notes for the Western Wall crevices (tradition) / 写有祈祷文的纸条放入西墙石缝（传统）
- [ ] Shabbat-appropriate supplies if visiting during Shabbat (pre-prepared food, no electronics) / 安息日期间的适当物资（预备食物，不用电子设备）
- [ ] Camera (but NOT on Shabbat in observant areas) / 相机（但在虔诚区域的安息日不可用）
- [ ] Kosher snacks if dietary observant / 如遵守饮食律法则携带洁食零食
- [ ] Sunscreen and water (Israel is hot and dry) / 防晒霜和水（以色列炎热干燥）

### What to Wear / 着装要求
- [ ] Men: kippah (head covering) at all religious sites / 男性：所有宗教场所须戴小圆帽
- [ ] Women: cover shoulders and knees; skirt preferred at Orthodox sites / 女性：遮盖肩膀和膝盖；正统场所首选裙子
- [ ] Ultra-Orthodox areas (Mea Shearim): very conservative — long sleeves, long skirts, no bright colors / 极端正统区域：非常保守——长袖、长裙、无鲜艳颜色
- [ ] No mix of linen and wool (for observant visitors) / 不混穿亚麻和羊毛（遵守者）

### What NOT to Do / 禁止事项
- [ ] Do NOT photograph on Shabbat in observant neighborhoods / 安息日不可在虔诚社区拍照
- [ ] Do NOT drive through ultra-Orthodox areas on Shabbat / 安息日不可驾车穿过极端正统区域
- [ ] Do NOT touch Torah scrolls / 不可触碰妥拉卷轴
- [ ] Do NOT extend hand to shake with ultra-Orthodox opposite gender / 不可向极端正统异性伸手握手
- [ ] Do NOT bring non-Kosher food to religious sites / 不可携带非洁食至宗教场所
